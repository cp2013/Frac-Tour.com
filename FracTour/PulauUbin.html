Enjoy Various Outdoor Activities at Pulau Ubin!

Discover beautiful scenery of Pulau Ubin with this exciting tour where you will trigger your own adventure spirit.

Pulau Ubin is the last village in Singapore. You can get the feel of Singapore in the 1960's and enjoy the pleasure of life in this island. You can start with strolling around this island and savor the freshness of wild life with the bicycle. You can also check out many kinds of animals and plants in various spots.

Kayaking is another way to enjoy your trip in this island. You could paddle through the sea and mangrove areas where you will find various insects, shrimps, mollusks, crabs, and plants.

Highlights :

  • Explore and learn about the ecological significance of the mangroves' flora and fauna
  • Kayak through island's mangroves and see varieties of wildlife such as crabs, jellyfish, mollusks, etc.
  • Pedal through different places in Pulau Ubin and learn about the local's culture and ceremonies.
  • Taste the famous seafood cuisine.
