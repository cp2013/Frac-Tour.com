Have A Taste of One of The Best Indonesian Delicacy in Semarang!

Semarang is located in the mid of Java Island. This city is well-known as the Spring Roll City, or in Indonesian Language "Kota Lumpia".

The Spring Roll in this city is especially delicious compared to other cities because it is filled with asparagus, little of fried eggs, and the most special sauce. This sauce could not be found on other places except on Semarang.

Another famous delicacy in this Semarang is its Soto. The most famous Soto in Semarang is Soto Bokoran. Soto Bokoran distinguished itself with the brown soup. You can try the Soto anytime, but it is better to visit the store in the morning because if you get there after lunch time, you will most likely to get the leftover.

There are many other kinds of foods that you can try in this city such as, Nasi Goreng Babat, Bandeng Presto, and Pig Sausages.

Highlights

  • Try out the Spring Roll of the famous Spring Roll City
  • You can also try out the Soto Bokoran
  • Nasi Goreng Babat, Bandeng Presto, and Pig Sausages are also recommended for you
